## Background

I'm Dani, an engineering enthusiast turned engineering student. Currently I'm focused on an electrical engineering pathway but I'm still considering my options. The academic achievements I'm most proud of is holding certifications in several industries such as technology, healthcare, law enforcement, and even disaster response. My previous engineering related experience centers around software engineering but I also have experience with car security systems and small engine repair. In my time at century college I was the founder and president of the social change club, served as Co-President of the LGBT Club, and was a member of the student senate.

## Interests

My reason for joining the engineering course was to help me decide if I want to pursue a career in engineering, and to have a more definitive and objective measure of how prepared I am academically. My passion for finding why and how things work has fueled my interest in engineering, but electrical engineering has always interested me because it seems almost like magic. Some of my dream improvements that I could work on with a better understanding of engineering are things like mesh networking, energy generation, and distributing agricultural production. I think that engineering can make a huge difference in everything from how we access information, healthcare, food, water, etc. I'm really excited to be a part of making that difference in the world.
